ABOUT THE STUDIO

Where Indian Craftsmanship Meets Modern Design

Founded by lead designer Jyoti A Nahata in Kolkata, Infinity Atelier is an editorial-class luxury interior design studio. We cultivate warm, modern environments that prioritize bespoke detail, tactile material palettes, and architectural order.

Recognized by Architectural Digest India and Elle Decor, our philosophy is anchored in collaborating with traditional Indian artisans to integrate custom masonry, woven cane details, and tailored timber joinery into minimal contemporary envelopes.

WHAT WE DO

Services Tailored to Your Space

Residential Interiors

Bespoke home designs built around your family's narratives. From luxury apartments to grand heritage estates, we deliver custom living layouts, storage, and material integration.

Commercial Spaces

Elevating corporate offices, boutique retail outlets, and hospitality hotspots with spatial efficiency and striking corporate identity branding that drives traffic and encourages productivity.

Furniture Design

Custom standalone pieces designed in-house and built by master woodworkers in India. Utilizing exotic hardwoods, cast metals, marble inlays, and customized woven structures.

Project Management

Seamless execution and end-to-end site oversight. We coordinate with engineers, lighting technicians, carpenters, and material vendors to bring blueprints to life with absolute precision.
